TITLE: Property Purchase Precautions
DOMAIN: PROPERTY
CATEGORY: Property Rights
TOPICS: property purchase, real estate, ownership verification, property documents, land dispute, buyer rights

OVERVIEW:
Property purchases involve significant financial commitments. Buyers should perform appropriate verification and due diligence before completing a transaction.

IMPORTANT CHECKS:

1. Ownership Verification
Confirm the seller's ownership rights.

2. Document Verification
Review relevant property documents.

3. Encumbrance Checks
Verify whether loans, disputes, or claims exist.

4. Physical Inspection
Inspect the property personally.

5. Agreement Review
Carefully review all contractual documents.

COMMON RISKS:

- Ownership disputes
- Fraudulent sales
- Missing approvals
- Encumbrances
- Boundary disputes

DOCUMENTS TO REVIEW:

- Ownership records
- Tax records
- Sale agreements
- Approval documents
- Identity documents

BEST PRACTICES:

- Verify information independently.
- Keep copies of all records.
- Maintain payment documentation.
- Seek professional guidance when necessary.
