Women Protection Rights India - Local Knowledge Note

Women facing harassment, domestic violence, stalking, workplace sexual harassment, dowry demands, or threats should prioritize safety and preserve evidence.

Evidence may include messages, call logs, photographs, medical records, workplace complaints, witness details, and dates of incidents.

Workplace sexual harassment complaints may involve an Internal Committee process where available.

Domestic violence situations may involve protection, residence, maintenance, custody, or compensation-related remedies depending on facts.
