TITLE: Workplace Harassment Awareness for Women
DOMAIN: WOMEN
CATEGORY: Women Rights
TOPICS: workplace harassment, women rights, employee safety, workplace misconduct, professional conduct, employment rights

OVERVIEW:
Women have the right to work in an environment that is safe, respectful, and free from harassment, intimidation, discrimination, and retaliation.

COMMON FORMS OF HARASSMENT:

- Unwanted comments
- Inappropriate messages
- Offensive jokes
- Repeated unwanted attention
- Intimidation
- Discriminatory treatment

WORKPLACE RIGHTS:

1. Right to Respectful Treatment
Employees should be treated professionally and respectfully.

2. Right to Report Concerns
Employees may report inappropriate behavior through established organizational channels.

3. Protection From Retaliation
Employees should not face retaliation for reporting genuine concerns.

4. Right to Confidentiality
Organizations should handle complaints responsibly and professionally.

WHAT TO DO:

- Record incidents.
- Save messages and emails.
- Identify witnesses if applicable.
- Follow company complaint procedures.

IMPORTANT DOCUMENTS:

- Emails
- Chat records
- Complaint submissions
- Witness information
