TITLE: Domestic Violence Support Information
DOMAIN: WOMEN
CATEGORY: Women Rights
TOPICS: domestic violence, family abuse, women rights, protection, personal safety, support services

OVERVIEW:
Domestic violence includes physical, emotional, verbal, psychological, financial, or controlling behavior within a family or domestic relationship.

COMMON EXAMPLES:

- Physical harm
- Threats
- Intimidation
- Financial control
- Isolation
- Emotional abuse

IMPORTANT RIGHTS:

1. Right to Safety
Every individual has a right to personal safety.

2. Right to Seek Assistance
Victims may seek support from family, support organizations, and appropriate authorities.

3. Right to Protection
Protection measures may be available depending on circumstances.

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:

- Seek a safe location.
- Contact trusted individuals.
- Preserve evidence.
- Maintain records of incidents.

IMPORTANT EVIDENCE:

- Photographs
- Medical records
- Messages
- Witness statements
