Senior Citizen Rights India - Local Knowledge Note

Senior citizens should preserve identity documents, medical records, property documents, bank records, messages, and details of neglect or abuse.

Issues may include maintenance support, neglect, financial exploitation, property pressure, abandonment, or abuse by relatives or caregivers.

Maintenance-related remedies may be relevant where a senior citizen cannot maintain themselves and qualifying relatives or legal heirs have responsibility.

Immediate safety, medical needs, and local authority support should be prioritized in abuse or neglect situations.
