Cyber Law India - Local Knowledge Note

Cyber incidents may include financial fraud, phishing, identity theft, account takeover, cyberstalking, impersonation, data misuse, and online harassment.

Users should preserve screenshots, URLs, phone numbers, email headers, transaction IDs, bank messages, app notifications, and timestamps.

For financial cyber fraud, fast reporting can be important. Record the complaint number and inform the bank or payment provider quickly.

Do not delete evidence before preserving copies. Avoid sharing OTPs, passwords, or remote access with unknown persons.
