TITLE: Cybercrime Complaint Process
DOMAIN: CYBER
CATEGORY: Legal Procedures
TOPICS: cybercrime complaint, online fraud, cyber reporting, digital crime, internet fraud, cybersecurity

OVERVIEW:
Victims of cybercrime may report incidents through official cybercrime reporting systems and local law enforcement channels where appropriate.

COMMON CYBERCRIMES:

- Online fraud
- UPI fraud
- Identity theft
- Cyber bullying
- Online blackmail
- Social media account compromise

STEP-BY-STEP PROCESS:

STEP 1:
Collect evidence related to the incident.

STEP 2:
Record transaction IDs, screenshots, URLs, and communication records.

STEP 3:
Submit a complaint through official cybercrime reporting channels.

STEP 4:
Keep complaint reference numbers for future follow-up.

STEP 5:
Cooperate with investigators if additional information is requested.

IMPORTANT EVIDENCE:

- Screenshots
- Emails
- Chat messages
- Transaction records
- Phone numbers
- Website URLs

BEST PRACTICES:

- Act quickly.
- Preserve evidence.
- Avoid deleting messages.
- Document all events chronologically.
