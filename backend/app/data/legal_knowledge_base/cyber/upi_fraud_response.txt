TITLE: UPI Fraud Response Guide
DOMAIN: CYBER
CATEGORY: Cyber Rights
TOPICS: UPI fraud, online scam, bank fraud, cybercrime, payment fraud, digital fraud

OVERVIEW:
UPI fraud occurs when criminals trick individuals into transferring money, sharing sensitive information, or approving fraudulent transactions.

COMMON FRAUD METHODS:

- Fake payment requests
- QR code scams
- OTP scams
- Customer care fraud
- Screen sharing scams
- Investment scams

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:

1. Contact the Bank
Report the unauthorized transaction immediately.

2. Report Through Official Channels
Submit a complaint through official cybercrime reporting mechanisms.

3. Preserve Evidence
Save screenshots, messages, transaction IDs, and call details.

4. Secure Accounts
Change passwords and review account activity.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION TO SAVE:

- Transaction ID
- Bank account details
- Screenshots
- Phone numbers used by scammers
- SMS messages

WHAT TO AVOID:

- Sharing OTPs
- Sharing PINs
- Installing unknown apps
- Clicking suspicious links
