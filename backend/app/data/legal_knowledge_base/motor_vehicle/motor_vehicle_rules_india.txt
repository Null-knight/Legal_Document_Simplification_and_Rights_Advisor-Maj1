Motor Vehicle Rules India - Local Knowledge Note

Road accident and traffic matters should be documented with photographs, vehicle numbers, insurance details, medical records, police records, and witness contact information.

Drivers should keep license, registration certificate, insurance, pollution certificate where applicable, and challan records.

Accident claims may involve injury records, disability records, repair bills, income proof, insurance policy, and police documents.
