TITLE: Tenant Responsibilities
DOMAIN: TENANT
CATEGORY: Tenant Rights
TOPICS: tenant obligations, tenant responsibilities, rent payment, rental agreement, property care, housing rules

OVERVIEW:
While tenants have important legal protections, they also have responsibilities under rental agreements and applicable laws.

COMMON TENANT RESPONSIBILITIES:

1. Pay Rent On Time
Rent should generally be paid according to the agreed schedule.

2. Maintain Cleanliness
Tenants should keep the property reasonably clean and sanitary.

3. Prevent Damage
Tenants should avoid intentional or negligent damage to the property.

4. Follow Agreement Terms
Rules specified in the rental agreement should generally be followed.

5. Report Problems
Major maintenance issues should be reported promptly.

COMMON VIOLATIONS:

- Non-payment of rent
- Unauthorized modifications
- Property misuse
- Illegal activities
- Unauthorized occupants

BEST PRACTICES:

- Maintain payment records.
- Read the agreement thoroughly.
- Notify the landlord about repairs.
- Communicate in writing whenever possible.

IMPORTANT DOCUMENTS:

- Rental agreement
- Rent receipts
- Maintenance requests
- Communication records
