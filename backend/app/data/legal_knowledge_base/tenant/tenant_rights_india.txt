Tenant Rights India - Local Knowledge Note

Tenants should preserve the rent agreement, rent receipts, deposit proof, repair requests, notices, and communication with the landlord.

Eviction usually should not happen by force. Written notice, lease terms, local rent control rules, and court or authority process may matter depending on the state and property type.

Security deposit deductions should be connected to rent due, damage beyond ordinary wear, or contract obligations. Tenants should ask for itemized reasons for deductions.

Essential repairs and basic habitability issues should be documented with dates, photographs, and written requests.
