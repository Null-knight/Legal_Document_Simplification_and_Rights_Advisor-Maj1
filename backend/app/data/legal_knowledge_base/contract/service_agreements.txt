Overview:

A service agreement is a legally binding contract between a service provider and a client. It defines the services to be provided, payment terms, responsibilities, timelines, and the expectations of both parties.

A written service agreement helps reduce misunderstandings and provides evidence if disputes arise.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Should every service agreement be in writing?

A:

A written agreement provides clear evidence of the agreed services, payment terms, and responsibilities, reducing the likelihood of future disputes.

------------------------------------------------------------

Q: Should I begin work before receiving an advance payment?

A:

That depends on the agreement. Many service providers prefer to begin work only after receiving the agreed advance payment.

------------------------------------------------------------

Q: Who owns the completed work?

A:

Ownership depends on the contract. Some agreements transfer ownership after full payment, while others grant only limited usage rights.
