RTI Rights India - Local Knowledge Note

RTI is used to request information held by public authorities. It is not a direct complaint mechanism for private disputes.

An RTI request should ask for specific records or information, identify the public authority, and avoid argumentative questions.

Applicants should keep filing proof, postal proof or portal acknowledgement, response dates, and appeal dates.

If no response or unsatisfactory response is received, the appeal path and limitation period should be tracked carefully.
