TITLE: Right to Information (RTI) Application Process
DOMAIN: RTI
CATEGORY: RTI Rights
TOPICS: RTI, right to information, government records, public information, transparency, citizen rights

OVERVIEW:
The Right to Information process allows citizens to request information from public authorities, subject to applicable laws and exemptions.

PURPOSE OF RTI:

- Increase transparency
- Improve accountability
- Access public information
- Understand government decisions

STEP-BY-STEP PROCESS:

STEP 1:
Identify the public authority that holds the information.

STEP 2:
Prepare a clear information request.

STEP 3:
Submit the application through the appropriate channel.

STEP 4:
Keep acknowledgment and reference details.

STEP 5:
Track the application status where available.

GOOD RTI PRACTICES:

- Ask specific questions.
- Avoid overly broad requests.
- Request information, not opinions.

COMMON REQUESTS:

- Project information
- Public spending details
- Recruitment information
- Government decisions
