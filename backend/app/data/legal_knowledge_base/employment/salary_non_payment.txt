TITLE: Salary Non-Payment Rights
DOMAIN: EMPLOYMENT
CATEGORY: Employment Rights
TOPICS: salary, wages, employment rights, non-payment, salary dispute, labor rights

OVERVIEW:
Employees generally have a right to receive wages or salary for work performed according to employment agreements and applicable labor regulations.

EMPLOYEE RIGHTS:

1. Right to Timely Payment
Employees are generally entitled to receive wages within agreed timelines.

2. Right to Salary Records
Employees should have access to salary statements or payment records.

3. Right to Raise Complaints
Employees may pursue grievance mechanisms when wages remain unpaid.

COMMON ISSUES:

- Delayed salary
- Partial salary payment
- Unauthorized deductions
- Withheld final settlement

WHAT EMPLOYEES SHOULD DO:

- Keep employment contracts.
- Preserve salary slips.
- Save bank transaction records.
- Maintain communication records.

IMPORTANT DOCUMENTS:

- Employment agreement
- Salary slips
- Bank statements
- Attendance records
