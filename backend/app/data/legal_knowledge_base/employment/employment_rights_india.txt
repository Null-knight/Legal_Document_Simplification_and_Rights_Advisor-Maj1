Employment Rights India - Local Knowledge Note

Employees should preserve offer letters, appointment letters, salary slips, attendance records, emails, appraisal letters, termination letters, and full-and-final settlement records.

Unpaid salary, illegal deduction, unsafe conditions, harassment, non-payment of statutory benefits, and abrupt termination are common employment-rights issues.

Termination analysis should check notice period, reason for termination, contract terms, company policy, and applicable labour law category.

Workplace complaints are stronger when dates, witnesses, written communication, and policy documents are available.
