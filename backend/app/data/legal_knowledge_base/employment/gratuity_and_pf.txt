TITLE: Gratuity and Provident Fund Rights
DOMAIN: EMPLOYMENT
CATEGORY: Employment Rights
TOPICS: gratuity, provident fund, PF, retirement benefits, employment rights, employee benefits

OVERVIEW:
Employees may become eligible for gratuity and provident fund benefits depending on employment duration, organizational policies, and applicable regulations.

COMMON BENEFITS:

1. Provident Fund
Provident Fund is a long-term savings benefit designed to support employees after retirement or during specific circumstances.

2. Gratuity
Eligible employees may receive gratuity after completing the required period of service according to applicable regulations.

3. Final Settlement Rights
Employees may request information regarding pending PF and gratuity claims during separation.

COMMON ISSUES:

- Delayed settlement
- Missing contributions
- Documentation problems
- Incorrect records

WHAT EMPLOYEES SHOULD DO:

- Verify PF records regularly.
- Keep salary slips.
- Maintain employment records.
- Request settlement statements when leaving employment.

IMPORTANT DOCUMENTS:

- Employment records
- Salary slips
- PF account details
- Separation documents
