TITLE: Warranty Claims
DOMAIN: CONSUMER
CATEGORY: Consumer Rights
TOPICS: warranty, repair, replacement, consumer rights, service center, product support

OVERVIEW:
A warranty is a commitment by the manufacturer or seller regarding the quality, performance, or repair of a product within a specified period.

CONSUMER RIGHTS:

1. Right to Warranty Service
Consumers may request repairs, replacement, or support according to warranty terms.

2. Right to Product Inspection
Service providers may inspect products to verify warranty eligibility.

3. Right to Information
Consumers should be informed about warranty coverage and exclusions.

COMMON WARRANTY ISSUES:

- Warranty denial
- Delayed repairs
- Unavailable spare parts
- Disputes regarding accidental damage
- Unauthorized service claims

WHAT CONSUMERS SHOULD DO:

- Keep warranty cards.
- Preserve purchase receipts.
- Follow usage instructions.
- Use authorized service centers.

IMPORTANT DOCUMENTS:

- Warranty card
- Invoice
- Service reports
- Repair records
