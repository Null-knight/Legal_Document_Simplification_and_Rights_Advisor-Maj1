TITLE: Consumer Complaint Process
DOMAIN: CONSUMER
CATEGORY: Legal Procedures
TOPICS: consumer complaint, consumer grievance, refund dispute, consumer rights, complaint process

OVERVIEW:
Consumers who face disputes involving products or services may seek resolution through customer support channels and formal consumer grievance mechanisms.

STEP-BY-STEP PROCESS:

STEP 1:
Contact the seller or service provider.

STEP 2:
Submit a written complaint describing the issue.

STEP 3:
Provide supporting evidence.

STEP 4:
Allow reasonable time for resolution.

STEP 5:
Escalate the matter through appropriate consumer grievance channels if unresolved.

EVIDENCE TO COLLECT:

- Invoice
- Screenshots
- Emails
- Photographs
- Service records

BEST PRACTICES:

- Remain factual.
- Maintain records.
- Communicate in writing.
- Organize documents.

COMMON COMPLAINTS:

- Defective products
- Refund disputes
- Service deficiencies
- Warranty issues
