TITLE: Misleading Advertising Rights
DOMAIN: CONSUMER
CATEGORY: Consumer Rights
TOPICS: misleading advertisement, false advertising, consumer rights, fake discount, deceptive marketing

OVERVIEW:
Consumers have a right to receive truthful and accurate information about products and services. Advertising should not intentionally deceive or mislead consumers.

COMMON EXAMPLES:

- False claims
- Hidden charges
- Fake discounts
- Exaggerated performance claims
- Misrepresentation of features

CONSUMER RIGHTS:

1. Right to Accurate Information
Advertisements should provide reasonably accurate descriptions.

2. Right to Fair Pricing
Pricing information should be transparent and not deceptive.

3. Right to File Complaints
Consumers may challenge misleading advertisements through appropriate channels.

WHAT CONSUMERS SHOULD DO:

- Save advertisements.
- Take screenshots.
- Keep purchase records.
- Document discrepancies.

IMPORTANT DOCUMENTS:

- Advertisement screenshots
- Purchase invoice
- Product descriptions
- Communication records
