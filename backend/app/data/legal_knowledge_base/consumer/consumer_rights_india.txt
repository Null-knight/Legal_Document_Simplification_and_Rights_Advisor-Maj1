Consumer Rights India - Local Knowledge Note

Consumers should keep invoices, warranty cards, screenshots, complaint numbers, emails, and delivery records.

Common consumer issues include defective goods, service deficiency, misleading advertisements, unfair trade practices, delayed delivery, overcharging, and refusal to honor warranty.

A consumer complaint should clearly state the product or service, date of purchase, amount paid, defect or deficiency, relief requested, and evidence attached.

Possible relief may include repair, replacement, refund, compensation, correction of unfair practice, or withdrawal of misleading advertisement depending on facts and applicable law.
