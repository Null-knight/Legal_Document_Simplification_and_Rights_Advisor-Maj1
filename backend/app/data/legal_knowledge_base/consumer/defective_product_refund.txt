TITLE: Defective Product Refund Rights
DOMAIN: CONSUMER
CATEGORY: Consumer Rights
TOPICS: refund, defective product, consumer rights, replacement, warranty, product complaint

OVERVIEW:
Consumers who receive defective, damaged, unsafe, or non-functional products may have rights to refunds, replacements, repairs, or compensation depending on the circumstances and applicable consumer protection laws.

CONSUMER RIGHTS:

1. Right to Quality Products
Consumers generally have a right to receive products that meet reasonable quality standards.

2. Right to Replacement
Defective products may qualify for replacement depending on seller policies and legal requirements.

3. Right to Repair
Certain products may be repaired instead of replaced.

4. Right to Refund
Refunds may be available when repair or replacement is not practical or reasonable.

COMMON DEFECTS:

- Manufacturing defects
- Physical damage
- Missing parts
- Safety issues
- Product not functioning as advertised

WHAT CONSUMERS SHOULD DO:

- Preserve invoices and receipts.
- Photograph defects.
- Contact the seller promptly.
- Maintain records of communication.

IMPORTANT DOCUMENTS:

- Invoice
- Warranty card
- Product photographs
- Complaint records
