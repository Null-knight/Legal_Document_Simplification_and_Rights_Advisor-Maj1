Sample tenant rights notes

A tenant normally has the right to quiet enjoyment of the rented property. This means the landlord should not enter without proper notice except in an emergency.

A lease may require written notice before either side ends the tenancy. The notice period depends on the lease terms and local law.

A landlord may be responsible for keeping essential services and basic habitability in working condition. Tenants should keep records of repair requests and responses.

Security deposit deductions should usually be connected to unpaid rent, damage beyond normal wear, or other lease obligations. Tenants should ask for an itemized explanation.
